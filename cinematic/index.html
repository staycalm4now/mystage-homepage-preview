Questions

Frequently asked

How does it work? expand_more
Three simple steps. First, you place your order through the brief form or pick a package. Next, our AI Agent builds a storyboard with images and sends it to you to review and approve. Once you approve the storyboard, we deliver your final video within 48 working hours (Mon-Fri).
Who owns the rights to the videos? expand_more
You hold 100% commercial ownership of every video we deliver. Use them on your site, ads, social, broadcast, anywhere.
How many revisions do I get? expand_more
Each package includes 1 storyboard revision. Edits in the final video are not supported unless it’s our mistake. Our AI Agent decides the creative direction; you review and adjust at the storyboard stage. For fully custom productions, contact us.
What's the delivery time? expand_more
48 working hours (Mon-Fri) from the moment you approve the storyboard. Most projects ship final inside a week of placing the order.
Can you match my specific brand colors and tone? expand_more
Yes. Send your brand guidelines, palette, and reference videos in the brief. We tune our models to your exact look.
Do I need to provide a script? expand_more
Not required. Share an idea, a product link, or a rough brief, and our team will draft the script with you.
Which languages do you support? expand_more
Most major languages are supported. Additional languages or vertical formats are charged extra. We’ll quote based on your delivery list.
Is the MyStage.ai platform the same as ordering a video from you? expand_more
No. They’re two different things. The videos on this page are ready-made productions our team creates for you from your brief, delivered in 48 working hours. The MyStage.ai platform is a credit-based, self-serve studio where you create everything yourself (text-to-image, image-to-video, text-to-video, music generation and more) using the latest AI models at very competitive prices. Plans start from $9/month. Pick us when you want polished done-for-you videos; pick the platform when you want to create unlimited content on your own.
